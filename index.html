The Problem

Emergency departments are overwhelmed by non-urgent cases, leading to long waiting times, staff burnout, and reduced quality of care.

Our Solution

Clear Path integrates telemedicine directly into emergency triage, redirecting Manchester Blue and Green patients to immediate remote care.

How It Works

Manchester triage is performed
Non-urgent patients identified
Telemedicine consultation begins
