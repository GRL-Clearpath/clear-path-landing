The Problem

Emergency departments are overwhelmed by patients who do not require urgent in-person care. This leads to long wait times, increased costs, and reduced quality of care for critical cases.

The Clear Path Solution

Clear Path integrates telemedicine directly into emergency triage workflows, enabling hospitals to safely redirect Manchester Blue and Green patients to immediate remote medical consultations.

Patient Journey (Manchester Blue & Green)

1. Arrival & Triage

Patient arrives at the emergency department and undergoes standard Manchester triage.

2. Non-Urgent Identification

Patients classified as Blue or Green are identified as non-urgent.

3. Telemedicine Redirection

Patient is redirected to an immediate telemedicine consultation.

4. Resolution or Referral

Case is resolved remotely or referred back to in-person care if necessary.

Value Proposition

For Hospitals

For Patients
